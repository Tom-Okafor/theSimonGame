THE SIMON GAME

Get ready to challenge your memory and reflexes with the classic Simon Game!
This iconic game tests your ability to repeat a randomly generated sequence of colors and sounds.

Simon starts with a simple sequence, then adds one color/sound each round, pushing your memory to the limit.
Repeat the sequence correctly to progress, but make one mistake and it's game over!

Originally released in 1978, Simon has remained a beloved game for generations, promoting cognitive development, hand-eye coordination, and healthy competition. Can you beat Simon?
